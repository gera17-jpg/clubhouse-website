About Us

The world-renowned 18 hole Jack Nicklaus design nestled amongst the majestic pine trees of Camp John Hay provides a breathtaking experience unlike any other. Breathing the cool, crisp mountain air while carefully maneuvering the hilly terrain will provide players of all abilities memories that will last a lifetime. The ONLY BENTGRASS GREENS in the country will prove to be the ultimate golfing challenge and are the things golfing lore are made of. Finally, the elegant log-architecture Clubhouse is the ultimate venue for relaxation and dining with its spectacular views and gardenlike setting.
